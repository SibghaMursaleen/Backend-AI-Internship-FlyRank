## 📌 Overview

This directory contains the deliverables for the **FL-01: What Are You Proving?** assignment. The goal of this assignment is to establish a strong **Proof Statement** defining the value proposition for potential clients (specifically **early-stage SaaS and AI founders**) and to articulate a clear **Thesis** justifying why a dedicated, high-quality project portfolio must exist to showcase frontend development skills and design decisions.

---

## ⚙️ Core Concepts

The core deliverables and statements of this assignment are defined below:

### 1. Proof Statement
> I help **early-stage SaaS and AI founders** launch their first professional landing page, creating a polished and credible online presence that builds trust from a customer's first impression. Through the projects in my portfolio, I demonstrate clean, scalable frontend development practices alongside thoughtful design, showing how I build websites that are both visually engaging and technically well-structured. If you're preparing to launch your startup and need a professional landing page that reflects the quality of your product, send me a message describing your project.

### 2. Thesis: Why This Portfolio Needs to Exist
*   **Demonstrating Quality**: A CV or LinkedIn profile can list technical skills and name past projects, but they cannot show the actual visual polish, user experience details, and clean structural execution of frontend code.
*   **Showcasing Design Decisions**: The portfolio acts as live proof of design rationale and frontend engineering capability, demonstrating to potential clients that projects are built intentionally and systematically.
*   **Building Trust**: Providing a polished, functional, and live portfolio builds immediate credibility and trust with founders from their very first interaction.
